Home page

I am creating a photo gallery to post projects and creative ideas that I want to share with a wide variety of people. These galleries will share my personal view of the world and my creative aspirations. It is a place where I can share different projects that I am working on,

Creating a gallery for a wide audience. I hope to be able to attract people who are interested in purchasing my photos to hang in their homes, I hope to attract people who see the paintings that I photograph and post in a gallery, these paintings will be available for sale, I hope to attract my family and friends to keep track of my projects.

I will use this site to link my Patrion site and other sites where people can purchase my images. I will also link another site that I have which is a site I use for booking clients for real estate photography

Sharing my personal view of the world, above all, I am using this site as a place to share the things that I create weather it is art or photos of the natural world, trips etc. This is a sight where I can just freely share since I have other sites that are specific to particular business or function

Share with my family and friends. I frequently have things that I want to share with my family and friends that gets them excited about the things that I am doing. I love the idea of having one single place that I can send them to be able to check out the things that I am working on.

I frequently have new projects that I work on, for example sometimes I will challenge myself to paint one painting every day and one large painting a week. I can easily send people there to see the daily progress. Currently I am working on a project that I felt inspired to do, that is to photograph every LDS chapel in Las Vegas, I have no Idea what I am going to do with them but I do know that as I do this, I can post some of the images to this site for my family friends and fallowers.

I am also considering putting up a blog post on this sight since I really enjoy writing about the things that cross my mind. Even so I am really struggling to come up with 600 words, it is true that I really am more inspired by images than by writing, so I think I may just have a space for a blog but maybe I wont post to it that frequently. Artist, Photographer

Hi my name is Lenny Stossel. I am a creative person who has no idea what I want, because what I want changes daily. This creats an interesting situation for an artist. Consider what it is like to work on a multy day project. This means I need to be increadibly flexible. Since I am also not super flexible, this creates frustration that may come out in just about anything that I create.

As a creative, I create compulsively and often without effort. I have to really try not to take something and turn it into something else. Even making food is like a journy of creative discovery for me. I usually don't cook the same thing twice. I may make a pie and If I really like it, I know that I have a good chance of liking the next cake I make.

Though rare, there are a few constants. For example, I always like chocolate, and if it is around it wont last long. Those constants tend to find their way into the things that I do. They are my, center of gravity, I suppose. They help to keep me sane and grounded.

Enjoy the journey
